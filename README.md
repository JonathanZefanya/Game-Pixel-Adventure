## Action
<b> Movement: </b> <br>
<b>W</b> to up <br>
<b>S </b> to down <br>
<b> A </b> to left <br>
<b> D </b> to right <br>
<b> Attack: </b> <br>
<b> SPACE </b> Pysichal Attack <br>
<b> C </b> Magic Attack <br>
<b> Change Weapon/Magic: </b> <br>
<b> Q </b> Weapon Change <br>
<b> E </b> Magic Change <br>
